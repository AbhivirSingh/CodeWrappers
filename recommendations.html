Workout and Nutrition Recommendations

Recommended Workouts:

  • 30 minutes of cardio (running, cycling)
  • Strength training (focus on major muscle groups)
  • Yoga or stretching sessions for flexibility

Nutrition Tips:

  • Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables.
  • Stay hydrated; aim for at least 8 glasses of water a day.
  • Limit processed foods and added sugars.
